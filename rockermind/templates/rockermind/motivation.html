About the site


All my life I have loved music, so when I read the specification of the last project of CS50 Web Programming with Python and JavaScript, I knew inmediatly that I had to do something related to music. One year ago, my estimated friend, and music dreamer, Andrés Padilla Solano, told me about an idea. He tought could be cool if would exist a page in where you were able to find concerts, maybe know some new music and promote the local underground scene. So, that was my inspiration for this site! I know it is too hard make a way in the music world, so this is my way of paying tribute to my good musician friends.


Please never stop dreaming!
